Polytope Listening Session 1
2025

Polytope Listening Session 1 is an experimental audiovisual Podcast exploring the multifaceted impact of nuclear weapons, intertwining historical resonances and contemporary implications. The installation draws upon various field recordings I made on a trip to Hiroshima in November 2024.

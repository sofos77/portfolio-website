Polytope Listening Session 1
2025

6 Channel Spatial Sound Installation

Polytope Listening Session 1 is an experimental audiovisual spatial podcast exploring the multifaceted impact of nuclear weapons, intertwining the bombing of Hiroshima and Nagasaki and current global happenings.

In 100cos(α) turn left
2024

Audiovisual Installation

rootstock, metal frame, holographic fans
1-channel video, 9216 x 1024px

In 100cos(α) turn left is an audiovisual installation that integrates distorted sound and image elements intended to symbolize digital alienation and the loss of orientation through digital navigation tools.
